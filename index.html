ESP32 Space Invader Duel

Prepare thy circuits and summon a friend—this isn't your grandma's Space Invaders~! Crafted as a diabolically delightful school assignment, ESP32 Space Invader Duel is a competitive 2-player arcade experience brewed upon the humble-yet-mighty ESP32 microcontroller ⚡👾
It reimagines the retro alien-zapping chaos with a twist of rivalry: two players, two screen, and a relentless battle for pixelated dominance!

Blasters ready~ Scores high~ Only the quickest thumbs shall claim glory in this cosmic skirmish of sparks and spite~! 💥🛠️✨

Sentient Combat Mechanic

What if you were the battle system? Not the hero, not the sword, not even the goblin—you're the combat mechanic itself! 🧠💥
In this turn-based RPG fever dream brewed for Pirate Software - Game Jam 16, you take control of the inner workings of the game's battle system. Every fight is yours to command—decide The Hero's moves, manage AP (Action Points), and guide them toward glory... or hilarious failure~ ⚙️👑

With a charmingly simple GUI and delightfully twisted premise, it's strategy, satire, and sentience all rolled into one~ 🎭✨
